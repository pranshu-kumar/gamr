How Does Optimo Work?

Sign-Up

Create an account with us. We have special plans available for corporate professionals, students and medical professionals. It is super-easy to register with us!

Set-Up

Once your account has been registered, you have to set up your account. Based on your affiliation to institutes or organizations, you can set up or attend meetings. The steps to do so are again very simple.

Meetings

Meeting Mode will let you both create and attend meetings. Once you enter a meeting and click the start meeting button, all you got to do is focus on your meeting and leave the rest to us. We will record every word, note down the important points discussed and document every task assigned. You can also schedule meetings and we will remind you when the meeting is near!

Study

In study mode, whenever you upload a document, we will analyze that document and prepare the document summary. We will also indentify the key themes and topics discussed along with definitions. We will also provide the best resources available in case you want to dig deeper. We also eliminate the useless and redundant information and recommend additional resources.
